Before-and-after architecture of a signal-driven B2B outbound engine, with unfinished work flagged upstream and downstream.

Case study · B2B recruitment agency (CZ/SK) · signal-driven demand capture

From spray-and-pray to a signal-driven outbound engine

The old motion blasted a cold CRM list and stalled from list exhaustion. The rebuild treats buying signals as the product: a warehouse ingests them, a scoring brain decides who + when, and a gate makes sure a human is never run over. Below: what it was, what it is now, and the honest unfinished edges on both ends.

BEFORE spray & pray

Volume-first. Three disconnected tools, manual lists, no memory.

  • Blast the whole CRM cold → ~2.6% reply; a mis-targeted trigger campaign → 0.17%
  • Campaign sat at ~0 sends for 9 days — list exhaustion (the real bottleneck)
  • 3 tools stitched by hand (list-builder + 2 senders), ~€800/mo
  • No signal capture, no scoring, no suppression — every send equal weight
  • CRM: 34% empty shells, duplicate & mother/daughter orgs, owner conflicts
  • Placement track record stuck in a manual spreadsheet

NOW signal-driven

One warehouse of truth. Signals scored, stacked, decayed, gated, routed.

  • Two first-party alpha signals: past-client re-sell + live hiring — un-buyable, the moat
  • Score = weight × time-decay × stacking; a single weak signal waits, a stack fires
  • Two lanes: cold → tiered sequences · hot → a human, before 9am
  • Suppression gate: open deal / recent human touch / do-not-contact / cooldown — deterministic, AI only for free-text notes
  • Validated market from national-register open data: 38k accounts, tiered by industry code
  • Runs 24/7 in the cloud; tool spend cut €800→€500

Scoring brain — who + when

decides; never sprays
score = base (past-client, no decay) + Σtype min( Σ weight×decay, cap )  ·  decay 1.5× (<24h) → 1.0× (2wk) → 0.3× (>30d)  ·  HOT ≥100 · WARM ≥50

Gate (deterministic): open deal · human touch ≤60d · do-not-contact · 14-day cross-channel cooldown → suppressed. AI only reads free-text notes for a buried "don't contact." Stacking means HOT structurally needs ≥2 independent evidences — no single-signal false alarms.

Honest unfinished edges — both ends

▲ Upstream — capture / supply

  • Hiring-post flow gated on a paid-scraper top-up (one external dependency)
  • Open-data vacancy ingest designed, not wired (the biggest, cleanest source)
  • Net-new tiering — resolve unknown companies → industry code → tier (register lookup, not yet automated)
  • Proof-bank still a spreadsheet — the credibility numbers that would sharpen copy
  • Competitive-posture layer designed but parked pending a lawyer's sign-off (unfair-competition law)

▼ Downstream — decision / close

  • Cold lane inactive — sequences built & tested, held until capture is live
  • Localized declension (name/role grammar) needs one config pass + spot-check
  • Feedback loop — no reweighting from outcomes yet (needs ~30–50 closes)
  • CRM consolidation — merge worklists generated; the human owner-assignment review is pending
  • Deliverability handshake (external email-verify → sequencer status) not wired
